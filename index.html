About Me

I'm a passionate data professional with over a decade of experience in the field. My career has evolved from traditional data analysis to a focus on business intelligence and analytics. I'm currently leading a team of analysts, providing valuable insights to drive data-driven decision-making.

My technical expertise includes proficiency in SQL, Python, and visualization tools like Tableau and PowerBI/SSRS. I'm dedicated to solving complex problems and empowering others to find effective solutions.

As I continue to grow in my career, I aspire to take on more leadership responsibilities and contribute to the further success of my organization.

Career

Over the years, I have worked at many locations. I have worked retail, I've worked for myself in landscaping, and have also spent 8+ years at the U.S. Post Office as a letter carrier.

Around 2013, I left the Post Office and took a job where I started to get into data and understanding how data drives processes and business decisions.

Most recently, I have been in the Business Intelligence field and am responsible for making sure business units have the reporting they need to complete their daily tasks, understand the key metrics of the business, and can make decisions based on prior history of a product or department.

Some of the key skills I have used in my career over the years include: Reporting; various languages including SQL, Python, R; Data Visualization skills; Data Analysis;
and Project Management
